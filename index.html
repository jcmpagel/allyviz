How to use AllyViz

  1. Enter a URL like wikipedia.org or https://en.wikipedia.org/
  2. Click "Check Accessibility" and wait approximately 2 minutes
  3. Explore results by hovering over highlighted elements

Shortcut: Type allyviz.com/ before any URL: allyviz.com/https://en.wikipedia.org/
